Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tutoring services, scheduling, and more.

EdForth Tutors is an online platform that connects students in the UAE with qualified, experienced, and vetted home tutors. We offer personalized, one-on-one tutoring in various subjects to help students achieve their academic goals. Simply browse through our list of courses, book a session, and our tutors will come directly to your home.

We offer tutoring in a wide range of subjects, including:

  • Mathematics (all levels)
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • English Language and Literature
  • Social Studies and History
  • Computer Science & Programming
  • Test Preparation (e.g., SAT, GCSE, IB, etc.)
  • And more!

If you need help in a subject that isn't listed, please contact us and we can help find a suitable tutor.

Booking a session is easy:

  • Browse through our courses.
  • Choose the subject, date, and time that works best for you.
  • Confirm the booking by completing the payment process.
  • Your tutor will arrive at your home at the scheduled time.
You can reschedule or cancel a session with at least 24 hours' notice without incurring any fees. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee. Please check our Cancellation Policy for more details.
Sessions are typically 1 to 2 hours long, depending on your preference. You can discuss the session duration with your tutor before the first class, and they will accommodate your child's learning pace.
Yes, most tutors provide learning materials, such as practice questions, notes, or exercises. Some may also recommend additional resources to support your child's learning. If you need specific materials, feel free to discuss it with your tutor.
If you are unsatisfied with the tutor's performance or the session, please contact us within 24 hours. We will do our best to resolve the issue, and in some cases, we may offer a partial or full refund, depending on the circumstances.
Yes, we have tutors experienced in working with students with special educational needs (SEN), including dyslexia, ADHD, and learning disabilities. Please contact us with specific requirements, and we'll match you with a tutor who has the right expertise.
Currently, we specialize in home-based tutoring, but we are exploring online tutoring options. If you're interested in remote tutoring, please contact us to inquire about availability.
